About

I came up with the idea to make my ezine for my cat after thoroughly thinking through my other choices.

In there were “the story of how I got my driving license”, “my favorite food” a few more and finally “the story of how I got my cat”.

Since this is so special to me, I decided to go with it.

I initially wanted to make some kind of yarn that the user can follow to reach the bottom, but I realized that was kind of hard to do with code.

P.S: Open the image in a new tab to be able to read through it!

In class we had to come up with a site map and how our homepage was going to be composed.

I made my site map consist of 5 pages.

I sketched the layout of the home page a few times before deciding to pick my final version.

In illustator I made the digital version of my home page, where even I created the logo.

P.S: Open the images in a new tab to be able to read through them!

These are the composite homepages I came up with.

I did them all digitally in illustator, while trying to diverge on several different looks and feels.

Ultimately I chose to have 6 pastel colors that tie in with the theme (I will use darker verisons for readability purposes) and for typography I picked Funnel.
